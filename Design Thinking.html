Design Thinking is a human-centered approach to problem-solving that encourages creativity, empathy, and experimentation. It helps individuals and teams create innovative solutions by deeply understanding user needs.

  • Empathize: Understand users through research and observation.
  • Define: Identify the core problems based on user insights.
  • Ideate: Brainstorm a wide range of creative solutions.
  • Prototype: Build simple models to test ideas quickly.
  • Test: Get feedback and refine based on user experience.
