Decision-Making Meetings

Effective decision-making meetings empower teams to evaluate options and choose the best course of action.

Key Types of Decision-Making

Consensus-Based

All members work together to find a mutually acceptable solution, fostering team unity and buy-in.

Majority Rule

Decisions are made through voting, where the option with the most votes wins. Efficient but may leave minority voices unheard.

Leader Decides

The team leader listens to feedback, then makes the final decision. Useful for time-sensitive situations.

Delegated Decision

A specific individual or subgroup is given the authority to decide, allowing focus and expertise to lead the outcome.
