SWOT Analysis is a strategic planning technique used to help organizations identify their:

  • Strengths: Internal factors that give an advantage (e.g., skilled workforce, strong brand).
  • Weaknesses: Internal factors that pose challenges (e.g., poor logistics, limited capital).
  • Opportunities: External factors to capitalize on (e.g., market expansion, tech trends).
  • Threats: External risks or competition that could hinder growth (e.g., economic downturns).

SWOT is widely used in business decision-making, marketing strategies, and personal career planning.
