Delegated decision-making is a leadership style where the authority to make a decision is passed on to an individual or a small team. The leader trusts the delegate(s) to act in the best interest of the group or organization.

  • Empowers Teams: Gives team members more responsibility and ownership.
  • Develops Skills: Helps grow leadership and critical thinking among staff.
  • Frees Up Leadership: Leaders can focus on higher-level strategy while others handle details.
  • Requires Trust: Works best when there is mutual trust and clear communication.
