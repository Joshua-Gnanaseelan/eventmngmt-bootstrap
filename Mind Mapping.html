Mind Mapping is a visual brainstorming technique that helps organize thoughts, ideas, or concepts around a central topic. It uses diagrams to represent tasks, keywords, or other items linked to a central concept, improving clarity and creativity.

  • Central Idea: Start with a main concept in the center of the map.
  • Branches: Create branches for related themes or subtopics.
  • Connections: Use lines and keywords to connect ideas logically.
  • Visual Elements: Add colors, icons, and images for better memory retention.
  • Applications: Ideal for planning, note-taking, studying, and problem-solving.
